What is Venue Center?

Venue Center is a platform where we can advertise Venue details for your every type of event, such as weddings, birthdays, mayon/mehndi, engagment, ameen, business parties, and many more. We can also show complete images of different sides of venue, as well as Location, facilities, price, guest accomudation, catering and booking dates of the venue. Here you can find Venues with your ease, either it is for hangout or any family event in a resonable price with respect to your choice of facilities you want.


Why I started Venue Center?

Pakistan is one of those coutries of world, where cultural events takes place to a great extend. Marriage in Pakistan relates to wedding traditional events, setup and followed by Pakistani people. Beginning with a number of events, wedding in Pakistan does not only culminate in a single day but spans over a week or two and in some families even more.
A part from wedding, many other cultural events, such as Eid Parties, Birthdays, Completion Of Quran Function, Business Parties, Prize Distribution Events and others, are also become a source of happiness and joy for Pakistanis. To fill the colours in these events, venue played a siginificant role. Banquet Halls, Lawns and Resturants are the best venues for these events.
Being a Pakistani, when I looked around myself in these events, I always thought something productive for the betterment of people of my country. So, I decided to start a Venue Center, where you can easily find venues for your event with complete details. You can booked it very easily just by using your mobile phone. Isn't it better for us to upgrade yourself and confirm your bookings on a website or mobile app? As the world is moving so rapidly towards the technology, so we will upgrading ourselves according to it. Thus, this realization led me to the formation of Venue Center.
